What Are Quadrants?

Quadrants are the four dividions of a graph dividied as such:

Quadrants
Ⅱ Ⅰ
Ⅲ Ⅳ

Note: Quadrants are always written in roman numerals.

Finding Quadrants

There's an easy way of determining quadrants just by looking at the coordinates, speficially whether the x and y are negative or not. Just use the table below:

Coord. Quad.
(x, y) Ⅰ
(-x, y) Ⅱ
(-x, -y) Ⅲ
(x, -y) Ⅳ
